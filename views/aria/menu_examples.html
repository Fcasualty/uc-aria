Welcome to The Career Center

All students and alumni are welcome to visit The Career Center and to take advantage of our services. Career counselors and health professions advisors are available for appoinmtnets. Attend a variety of workshops to obtain valuable information about how to conduct your job search, writing a winning resume, brushing up your interviewing skills, how to make an impressionat a career fair and many other topics. Visit the Resource Center to read books, do research, or for drop-in and research services.
